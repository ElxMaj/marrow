# Interview: PFC Gdynia front desk

Interviewer: walk me through how staff log in at the front desk.

Staff: honestly, we share one login at the desk. The password ends up written on a post-it stuck to the monitor because three people use the same terminal across a shift and nobody wants to retype it twenty times an hour.

Interviewer: that sounds risky. What did you land on?

Staff: we decided magic links, no shared passwords. A member taps their email, gets a link, and they are in. Nobody writes anything down anymore, and we can see who actually did what at the desk.

Interviewer: what about speed between shifts?

Staff: open question really, how fast must desk staff re-authenticate between shifts without it being annoying. We did not settle that yet.
